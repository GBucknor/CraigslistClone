The Henry Public House is currently looking for qualified individuals to join our team.

- Line Cooks

We are looking for energetic, outgoing people with kitchen experience to join our team and be a part of the fastest growing hospitality company in British Columbia.

Work with some of Canada's best Chefs, including our Director of Culinary Operations and Top Chef Canada Season 2 winner, Chef Matthew Stowe, and Corporate Chef and MasterChef Canada Season 3 winner, Chef David Jorge.

If you enjoy creating food, kitchen culture and creating memorable experiences there is no better place to be! Our company provides tremendous growth opportunities as we are continually expanding our collection of concepts.

The ideal candidate has:
• Great work ethic
• Ability to work independently as well as part of a team
• Food safe level 1 (or be willing to get certified)
• Attention to detail
• Strong communication skills and able to multi-task under pressure
• The ability to work in a fast paced environment
• A willingness to learn
• A legal entitlement to work in Canada
• Schedule open availability for evenings, weekends and holidays.

What we offer
• Exciting career opportunities
• Competitive wages
• Flexible hours
• Fun and exciting work environment
• Opportunity for growth

Key Responsibilities:
• Food handling
• Cleanliness
• Maintain quality of food
• Prepare and cook menu items to specifications
• Follow daily cleaning duties

Please reply to this email with your resume attached and only select candidates will be contacted for an interview.
